Grammar Corner

Key grammar topics with rules and artificial intelligence examples.

Structure: will + base verb.

Examples:

  1. Artificial intelligence will become a normal part of manufacturing, banking, retail, and transportation.La inteligencia artificial se convertira en una parte normal de la manufactura, la banca, el comercio minorista y el transporte.
  2. Companies will install AI systems for supply chains, customer service, and healthcare diagnostics.Las empresas instalaran sistemas de IA para cadenas de suministro, atencion al cliente y diagnosticos de salud.
  3. The software segment will hold almost 45% of the AI market by 2026.El segmento de software tendra casi el 45% del mercado de IA para 2026.
  4. New processors will become faster and more efficient in the next few years.Los nuevos procesadores seran mas rapidos y eficientes en los proximos años.
  5. Systems engineers will close old manual processes and design digital infrastructure with AI tools.Los ingenieros de sistemas cerraran procesos manuales antiguos y disenaran infraestructura digital con herramientas de IA.

Structure: am/is/are going to + verb.

Examples:

  1. The global AI market is going to grow from about $539 billion in 2026 to more than $3 trillion by 2033.El mercado global de IA va a crecer de aproximadamente 539 mil millones de dolares en 2026 a mas de 3 billones de dolares para 2033.
  2. Data center power consumption is going to nearly double by 2030.El consumo de energia de los centros de datos casi se va a duplicar para 2030.
  3. Hardware companies are going to install more efficient GPUs and TPUs.Las empresas de hardware van a instalar GPUs y TPUs mas eficientes.
  4. Cloud providers are going to close inefficient data processes and improve chip design.Los proveedores de nube van a cerrar procesos de datos ineficientes y mejorar el diseno de chips.
  5. AI is going to change how engineers create, install, and maintain digital systems.La IA va a cambiar la forma en que los ingenieros crean, instalan y mantienen sistemas digitales.

Use: can, should, must, may.

Examples:

  1. AI can recognize images and make decisions using algorithms and machine learning.La IA puede reconocer imagenes y tomar decisiones usando algoritmos y aprendizaje automatico.
  2. Companies should install AI responsibly in customer service and healthcare diagnostics.Las empresas deberian instalar IA responsablemente en atencion al cliente y diagnosticos de salud.
  3. Data centers must close unnecessary tasks to reduce electricity consumption.Los centros de datos deben cerrar tareas innecesarias para reducir el consumo de electricidad.
  4. New processors may help AI systems become faster and more efficient.Los nuevos procesadores pueden ayudar a que los sistemas de IA sean mas rapidos y eficientes.
  5. Systems engineers should install updates and understand both software and hardware requirements for AI.Los ingenieros de sistemas deberian instalar actualizaciones y comprender los requisitos de software y hardware para la IA.

Structure: have/has + past participle.

Examples:

  1. Artificial intelligence has changed how companies analyze information.La inteligencia artificial ha cambiado la forma en que las empresas analizan informacion.
  2. Machine learning has helped software copy some aspects of human thinking.El aprendizaje automatico ha ayudado al software a copiar algunos aspectos del pensamiento humano.
  3. The demand for GPUs and TPUs has increased because AI needs powerful hardware.La demanda de GPUs y TPUs ha aumentado porque la IA necesita hardware potente.
  4. Cloud providers have installed new data centers to support AI services.Los proveedores de nube han instalado nuevos centros de datos para apoyar servicios de IA.
  5. Some companies have closed old systems because AI has become an important business tool.Algunas empresas han cerrado sistemas antiguos porque la IA se ha convertido en una herramienta empresarial importante.

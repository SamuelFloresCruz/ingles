Grammar Corner

Key grammar topics with rules and artificial intelligence examples.

Structure: will + base verb. Example: AI will transform many professions.

Structure: am/is/are going to + verb. Example: We are going to create an AI presentation.

Use: can, should, must, may. Example: Developers must use AI responsibly.

Structure: have/has + past participle. Example: AI has changed digital communication.

Structure: be + past participle. Example: Data is processed by an AI model.

Grammar gives structure to ideas.

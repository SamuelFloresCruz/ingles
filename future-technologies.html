AI Technologies

Definitions and predictions about artificial intelligence and future innovations.

Machine Learning

Machine learning is a branch of AI that allows computer systems to learn patterns from data and improve their performance without being programmed step by step.

Prediction: Machine learning will be used to personalize education, detect diseases earlier, and improve smart services.

Natural Language Processing

Natural language processing is an AI field that helps computers understand, generate, and translate human language.

Prediction: Chatbots and translators will become more natural, accurate, and useful for global communication.

Computer Vision

Computer vision allows machines to interpret images and videos, recognizing objects, people, movement, and visual patterns.

Prediction: Computer vision will support autonomous vehicles, security systems, medical diagnosis, and industrial quality control.
